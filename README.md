Project Title:   Sales Performance Dashboard using Power BI
Objective:   To build a dynamic and interactive dashboard in Power BI that helps    	       	               visualize and analyse sales performance data across regions, time 		         	               periods, and product categories.
Scope:	Sales data forecasting by Order Date, shipping mode
		Sales data visualization by segment, category, subcategory, segment
		KPIs: Total Sales, Quantity Sold, Total Profit, Shipping days 
		The map visual effectively highlights geographic performance, showing the sum of Sales and Profit by State.
Data Sources:  Superstore_Sales_Dataset.xlsx – Contains order-level details                                     	                    Data Range: Jan 2019-Jan 2021  
Tools & Technologies:   Power BI Desktop, Microsoft Excel, DAX, Power Query
Dashboard Features:
Pie Charts: Sum of Sales by Segments, Sum of Sales by Payment Mode
Clustered Bar Chart: Sum of Sales by Category, subcategory, ship mode
Area Chart: Sum of Profit, Sum of Sales by month, order date 
Line Chart: Sum of Sales by Order Date
Card: Sales by Regions 
Map: Sum of Sales, Profit by State
KPI Cards: Total Sales, Total Profit, Total Quantity, Total Shipping days.
Data preparation: Sampled Top 100 rows from ‘Superstore_Sales_Dataset’ using DAX to explore structure and validate data consistency before applying transformations.
Key Insights: A 15-day sales forecast was implemented using Power BI's built-in forecasting capabilities to predict short-term sales trends.
		       The Standard Class shipping mode accounts for the majority of orders, making it the most frequently used shipping method among customers.
		       The highest single-day sales were recorded on 2nd September 2020, with a total of $11,778.85 in revenue.
		       The West region recorded the highest overall sales, with a total revenue of $522,000, a profit of approximately $68,000, and a total quantity sold of 7,298 units.
